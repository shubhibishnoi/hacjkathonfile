About Us

Empowering Women Through Financial Inclusion

build a future where women thrive, businesses grow, and economies prosper. Our mission is to expand the economic assets, participation, and power of low-income women and their households by helping them access financial services, knowledge, and markets. Globally, women-owned MSMEs (micro, small & medium enterprises) make up 38% of all MSMEs and are more likely to be smaller, informal, and home-based. While two out of three businesses are owned by men, women’s entrepreneurship is growing; there are 10 million women-owned businesses in developing countries alone. Yet, 70% of women entrepreneurs report inadequate access to growth capital. For example, women-owned MSMEs specifically face higher obstacles to accessing credit and financing from financial institutions, because they are “thin file”—meaning they have limited credit history or formalized business documentation.
